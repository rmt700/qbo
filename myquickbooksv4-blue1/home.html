Overview

According to the US Chamber of Commerce, one of the reasons businesses fail is due to cash flow problems. Understanding the financial health of your business is key to whether your business will succeed and grow, or decline. QuickBooks is a proven accounting software that will help you organize your company's transactions and produce meaningful reports for analysis or distribution to 3rd parties.

Questions to Ask

Some basic questions to ask:

  • Can the business meet its financial obligations?
  • Is the business profitable?
  • Is there free cash to invest and grow your business?
Mission

Our mission is to help entrepreneurs, not-for-profit organizations and small business owners navigate and master QuickBooks, to produce quality reporting for sound decision making.
